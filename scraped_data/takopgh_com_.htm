Step into the extraordinary world of Tako, located in downtown Pittsburgh, where the flavors of Mexico and Japan unite in a mesmerizing fusion of street food delights. Our innovative restaurant presents a truly unparalleled blend of both cuisines, crafting a menu that tantalizes taste buds and leaves patrons in awe of its boundless creativity.

But Tako is more than just a culinary adventure; it’s a haven for tequila and mezcal enthusiasts. With an unmatched selection sourced locally for the highest quality, our knowledgeable staff is eager to guide both aficionados and newcomers through our impressive collection, elevating your dining experience to extraordinary heights.

At Tako, our dedication to sourcing the freshest, finest local ingredients reflects a genuine commitment to sustainability and support for local farmers and producers. This conscious approach to dining adds an extra layer of appreciation for the exceptional culinary journey that awaits you with each visit.

In essence, Tako transcends the definition of a mere restaurant; it’s an immersive experience celebrating the seamless convergence of two diverse yet harmonious culinary worlds. Prepare yourself for a remarkable taste adventure, where the brilliance of Tako’s chefs shines through, and the soulful marriage of Mexican and Japanese influences takes center stage. For an unforgettable dining escapade that promises to linger in your memory, Tako is the name you’ll fondly remember.
