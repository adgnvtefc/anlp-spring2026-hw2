Pittsburgh Will Surprise You

Keep warm with seasonal favorites in our city of multitudes

Ready or not, winter is upon us, but just because it's cold outside doesn't mean Pittsburgh isn't heating up with fun! Enjoy our plethora of winter outdoor activities, treat yourself to a relaxing weekend, and warm up at cozy coffee shops sure to make you melt.

Experience an unforgettable February in the city!

Love is in the air this February, and Pittsburgh has everything you need to celebrate Valentine's Day.

If you haven't booked your dinner reservations, we have a complete list of romantic dinners to treat your sweetheart to. Spoiler alert: none of the options are bad options. We have everything from a fancy dinner overlooking the Golden Triangle to takeout tacos perfect for homebodies. Of course, Valentine's Day isn't just about romantic love. Celebrate your favorite friends for Galentine's Day with brunch, mimosas, candle making, and more. And for those still looking for that special someone, Pittsburgh is the perfect place to live out your romantic comedy dreams. Here's our feel-good guide you can use to turn your life into a Hallmark Channel movie
