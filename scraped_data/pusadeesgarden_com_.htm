FAQs

Important notes
• No outside food or beverages are permitted.
• We do not have boosters or highchairs. Please include children in your guest count when reserving a table.
• We do not offer take out.
• We cannot combine multiple reservations to create larger parties.
How do I make a reservation?

To make a reservation at Pusadee’s Garden, use our reservation app: resy.com

When do reservations open?
Reservations are opened one day at a time, thirty days in advance at 7am. All availability is listed on Resy, we do not hold reservations to be made over the phone or in person. We also have some walk-in availability each night.
What should we do if we want to walk in?
Walk-ins are offered nightly and are first come first serve. Upon arrival, our host will let you know what the expected wait time is and can add you to the list for tables or bar seats. We are unable to add tables to the waitlist over the phone, as waits can change quickly.
How do I get a spot outside in the garden?

When making a reservation, please add a note in your reservation that garden seating is preferred. Advance reservations will have first priority for garden tables in order of reservation date, but cannot be guaranteed. We will make every attempt to honor requests, but when garden seating is full or if the weather is prohibitive, the reservation will remain as an indoor dining table.

How does the weather impact garden seating?

If the weather is prohibitive, the garden seating area will by closed. We do not have outdoor heaters or rain shelter. Closures are decided day-of.

I have a party with more than 8 people, but Resy isn’t showing me any times. Can I book over the phone?
We are unable to accommodate parties larger than 8. All of our reservations are individual tables, and we are unable to push multiple tables together to create additional large parties. If the party size you are looking for does not have availability on Resy, that means that we are fully booked for that party size on that day. You can add yourself to the Notify list on Resy to be notified if any parties cancel.
Can I host a private event, buyout, baby shower, rehearsal, or wedding at Pusadee’s Garden?
We cannot accommodate parties larger than 8, and can only accommodate standard dinner reservations. We do not offer buyouts or private events in order to offer the most availability to our community as possible.
Can we bring a cake or BYOB?
We do not permit outside food or beverages. If you are celebrating a birthday, please let the host/server know and we can add a candle to any dessert if you are interested in ordering one in our restaurant. We are not BYOB.
Is the patio pet friendly?
Pets are not permitted on our patio.
How can I find out if someone cancels or a table becomes available?
If you are unable to find a reservation that suits you, we recommend adding yourself to the Notify list on Resy. You can personalize the notification to times and party sizes that interest you, and Resy will send you an email if a table becomes available. If you receive an email from Resy, please follow the link within the email to book. If the table is no longer available upon opening the link, someone has already rebooked that table. The notify list is not a waitlist, and you will not be notified throughout the night for walk-in tables through this list.
What should I do if I'm running late or can't make it?

Please notify us if you are running late. Reserved tables will be held for 15 min. After 15 minutes, we will offer your table to a walk-in guest and cannot guarantee a table. Please call or text if you will be late and we will do our best to accommodate you. A cancellation fee of $25/person will apply if not cancelled at least 24 hours prior to reservation.
