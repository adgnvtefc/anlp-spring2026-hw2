Schedule of Events

Get ready for a weekend full of fun, flavor, and festivities! The Great American Banana Split Celebration is a free, family-friendly celebration featuring something for everyone:

Live Entertainment Music, performances, and stage shows all weekend long

Kids Activities Crafts, games, and interactive fun for the little ones

5K Banana Run Race, jog, or cheer on runners in this community favorite

Car Show Classic, vintage, and unique vehicles on display

Baking Contest Sweet treats and creative desserts compete for top honors

Banana Eating Challenge A fun, fast-paced contest you dont want to miss

The Story of the All-American Dessert that Started in Latrobe

It started in 1904 with an experiment by a local man, David Strickler. Today it is the official dessert of Pennsylvania and an iconic part of American culture. The Great American Banana Split Celebration has been a staple in Latrobe since 2013 and, this year we celebrate its 12-year anniversary!
