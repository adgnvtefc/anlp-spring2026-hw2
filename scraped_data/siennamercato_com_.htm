    Sienna Mercato – Downtown Pittsburgh’s Premier Multi-Level Italian Dining Destination
    Discover Sienna Mercato, a one-of-a-kind Italian restaurant experience in the heart of downtown Pittsburgh in the Cultural District. Our three unique floors offer something for every craving—from handcrafted meatballs and wood-fired pizza to elevated Italian classics made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Enjoy craft cocktails and an award-winning rooftop beer garden with stunning skyline views. Perfect for date nights, happy hour, family gatherings, private events, and group dining, Sienna Mercato brings together vibrant atmosphere, exceptional service, and unforgettable flavors—all under one roof.

    Explore the Menus at Sienna Mercato – Italian Dining in Downtown Pittsburgh
    From signature gourmet meatballs and wood-fired pizza to house-made charcuterie, fresh pasta, and craft cocktails, Sienna Mercato offers a menu for every mood. Dine through three distinct concepts, including our rooftop beer garden with sweeping Pittsburgh skyline views. Whether you’re planning lunch, happy hour, dinner, or a night out, discover chef-driven Italian favorites made with quality ingredients and bold flavors. Browse the menus for each floor and find your next craving.

    Private Events

    Host Unforgettable Private Events at Sienna Mercato – Downtown Pittsburgh
    Plan your next celebration at Sienna Mercato, downtown Pittsburgh’s premier three-story event venue. With versatile private and semi-private spaces, customizable Italian-inspired menus, and dedicated event planning, we create seamless experiences for weddings, rehearsal dinners, corporate gatherings, holiday parties, showers, and social celebrations. From our vibrant rooftop beer garden to elegant indoor dining rooms, your guests will enjoy exceptional cuisine, craft cocktails, and standout service in a one-of-a-kind atmosphere.
