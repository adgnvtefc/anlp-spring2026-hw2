    Reynolds' Blend
    Say hello to our 2025-2026 year-long donation blend! This exciting coffee is presented in collaboration with Bryan and Blair Reynolds. Proceeds from Reynolds' Blend benefit Lending Hearts, a Pittsburgh-based nonprofit providing emotional, social and physical support to children living with cancer and their families/caregivers.
     
    Much like Bryans professional baseball career as an all-star outfielder, this blend aims to empower and inspire. Bryans ideal cup of coffee is fruity, acidic, floral, and light. These descriptors can often describe coffees from across the African continent. As such, that is where our initial search for blend components started.
     
    As this blend changes and evolves over the next year, expect to enjoy distinct African coffees from countries including Ethiopia, Rwanda, and Uganda on deck and in the hole to hit this blend out of the park!
